About Us

Managed by 4 young passionate pet lovers, we understand the bond between owner and pet, the care that the pet needs, therefore LeleLand Pet Grooming Services is born. Maintaning a well hygiene health for our pet will defintely increase the emotions well-being for them as well as owner. Our Spa Treatment is the preferred choice for majority pet's owner among our renowned services as this include deep coat cleansing, skin ailments and even nourishing treatments for smoother and manageable coats. Don't forget, WE ARE PET LOVERS TOO!
